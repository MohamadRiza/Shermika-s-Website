Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to queries regarding strategic workshops, aviation slots, and academic modules.

What consulting services do you offer?

Dr. Shermika Kuweju offers strategic B2B advisory services focused on tourism promotion campaigns, joint destination cross-marketing, commercial aviation route development, slot optimizations, and corporate brand management.

Can you assist airlines with regional regulatory approvals?

Yes. Having served as the Regional Manager for Turkish Airlines, District Manager for Oman Air, and Senior Supervisor for Thai Airways across Sri Lanka and the Maldives, she has extensive experience working with Civil Aviation Authorities to secure route capacity, direct operations, and fifth-freedom traffic rights.

Are you available for international lecturing and seminars?

Yes, she is an active lecturer in Sales, Marketing, and Brand Management. She conducts executive masterclasses, university module guest lectures, and tailored corporate boardroom workshops globally.

How can we organize a custom destination marketing workshop?

Please fill out the contact form selecting "B2B Tourism Marketing Consulting." Include details about your organization (e.g. DMC, hotel chain, embassy, or airline) and proposed dates. We will coordinate a feasibility slot to structure a custom brief.
