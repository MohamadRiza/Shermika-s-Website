Student Experience

Living in Baku

Explore the key metrics of student life, logistics, and cost-effective living expenses in Azerbaijan's capital.

Tuition Fees

Enjoy highly competitive tuition rates. English-taught undergraduate and postgraduate degree programmes are priced very affordably compared to Western Europe, delivering global standards at a highly accessible cost.

Accommodation

Host universities provide secure, modern student dormitories directly on or near campus. Alternatively, private flat shares and student apartments are widely available and very affordable across Baku's residential areas.

Living Expenses

Baku is renowned as a budget-friendly capital city for international students. Daily expenses for dining out, supermarkets, high-speed mobile internet, and metro/bus travel are highly cost-effective and student-friendly.

Student Visas

The entry visa process is fully digitized via the official e-visa portal. Upon arrival in Baku, your host university assists in submitting applications for your official State Migration Service student residence permit.
